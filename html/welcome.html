The National Society of Professional Insurance Investigators (NSPII) has created a charitable giving foundation called the NSPII Foundation. Its Mission Statement is as follows:

Formed in 1983, the National Society of Professional Insurance Investigators (NSPII) is a non-profit organization dedicated to furthering investigative ideas, procedures and techniques. NSPII sponsors annual, national and chapter seminars, publishes a newsletter, and conducts regular meetings to inform and develop professionals in the areas of insurance fraud recognition, prevention and investigation.
